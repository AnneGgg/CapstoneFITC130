Forms of Loss

Loss can come in many forms

  • Loss of a loved one
  • Loss of a relationship
  • Loss of a lifestyle

My experience is about the loss of a loved one.

After the Loss

In the beginning, it was very important to focus on simple tasks to keep me distracted and to help move into my new reality.

My basic routine became:

  1. Get up
  2. Get dressed
  3. Eat breakfast
  4. Go outside, preferably for a walk
  5. Eat supper

Every day, I made sure to at least do these things. Eating was so hard, food was tasteless; I have not eaten cardboard, but I am sure that is similar to what it was like to eat. It took a long time for food to actually have noticeable flavour again.

Going outside everyday helped a lot - it was a distraction from my surroundings, as well as a chance for life to feel normal again. Some days, walking felt like the best thing to do, so I would go on extra walks.

Getting up and getting dressed seem pretty basic, but in my state, I would have probably lived in my pajamas. Doing this also helped life feel a little more normal.
